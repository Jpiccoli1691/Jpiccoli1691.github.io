"Massano & Piccoli_ Breakout game"

In the game "Breakout," You are the paddle trying to escape the space. It would be best if you broke all the blocks to do that. You must smash the ball against the walls to break the bricks, but don't let the ball get pasted you, or you will lose a life if you lose all your lives; the game is over, and you need to restart.

Hugo Massano and Jonathan Piccoli coded this game.

The game's object is to break all the blocks before you run out of lives. To win, you must reach a screen of 20.
